BBA - Bachelor of Business Administration

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a 3-year undergraduate program focused on business management principles, leadership, and organizational skills. It prepares students for careers in corporate management, marketing, and finance.

Course Highlights:

  • Principles of management
  • Marketing and sales strategies
  • Financial accounting
  • Human resource management
  • Business communication
